My Bio

My name is Eric, and I'm a former NSCA certified personal trainer. One of my passions is biomechanics, and how knowledge of how the body moves can be used to make your life easier through training!

There are a lot of myths out there in the fitness world.
There are a lot stylish sites that make promises that sound too good to be true.
There are a lot of influencers vying for clicks without offering anything of substance.
My goal here is to help you understand how training can help you, through real science, and honest advice.
